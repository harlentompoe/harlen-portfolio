Our Pride

National Anthem

All hill Liberia, hill! (All hill)
This glorious land of Liberty
Shall long be ours.

Though new her name,
Green be her fame,[A]
And mighty be her power

In joy and gladness
With our heart united,
we'll shout the freedom of race benighted

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious Liberty,
by God's command!

All hail Liberia, hail!(All hail)
In union strong success is sure
we cannot fail

With God above
our right to prove
we will o'er prevail

With heart and hands
our country's cause defending
We'll meet the foe
With valor unpretending.

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious Liberty
God's command!
